Learning Steps

  • Start with basic posture (Araimandi)
  • Practice Mudras daily
  • Follow the correct sequence of steps

Safety First

  • Always warm up before starting
  • Keep your back straight to avoid strain
  • Stay hydrated during practice

Cultural Respect

  • Respect the traditions and Guru
  • Wear appropriate practice costume
  • Understand the stories behind the art
